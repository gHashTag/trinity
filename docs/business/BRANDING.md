## Name & Meaning

| Element | Value | Meaning |
|---------|-------|---------|
| **Network** | Trinity Network | Decentralized AI inference platform |
| **Token** | $TRI | Native utility token |
| **Symbol** | Trinity = 3 | Ternary system {-1, 0, +1} |

### Why "Trinity"?

1. **Mathematical**: Trinity = 3 = Ternary arithmetic
2. **Technical**: Three states {-1, 0, +1} for balanced representation
3. **Philosophical**: Golden identity φ² + 1/φ² = 3
4. **Memorable**: Simple, universal concept

### Why "$TRI"?

1. **Short**: 3 characters, easy to type
2. **Clear**: TRI = Trinity = Three
3. **Unique**: Not commonly used ticker
4. **Searchable**: Easy to find on exchanges

## Voice & Tone

### Principles

1. **Technical but accessible** - Explain complex concepts simply
2. **Confident but not arrogant** - We have real advantages
3. **Community-focused** - Users are partners, not customers
4. **Forward-looking** - Building the future of AI

### Do's

- ✅ "Run AI on your CPU"
- ✅ "Earn $TRI for your compute"
- ✅ "No GPU required"
- ✅ "Decentralized and censorship-resistant"

### Don'ts

- ❌ "Revolutionary" / "Game-changing" (overused)
- ❌ "To the moon" / "WAGMI" (unprofessional)
- ❌ Technical jargon without explanation
- ❌ Promises of specific returns
